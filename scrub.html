How to Use +

For optimal nail growth, apply a small amount to clean, dry nails. For best results, use on unpolished nails to reach the nail bed; if nails are polished, focus on the cuticle area.

  • Apply to each nail individually as needed.
  • Recommended to use twice daily to maximize growth and health.
  • Gently massage into each nail and surrounding skin for 30–60 seconds to ensure thorough absorption.
  • Wipe off any excess oil after application.
